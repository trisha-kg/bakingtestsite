Chia Seed Pudding

Source: omisido

Image by omisido (Pixabay License)

Ingredients: 2 tablespoons of chia seeds, 1/2 cup of milk of choice, 1 teaspoon honey, cut fruits (optional)

Step 1: Pour ingredients into a jar and mix well. Let settle for 2-3 minutes then mix again very well until you see no clumping.

Step 2: Cover the jar and store in fridge overnight or for at least 2 hours.

Step 3: When you're ready to eat it, top with your favorite fruit and enjoy cold!
